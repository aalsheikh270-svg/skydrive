About Us

From a childhood passion for sports to building a community-focused sporting goods business

Our Journey: From Passion to Performance

From the moment our founders could walk, they had a ball at their feet. Sports wasn't just something they did—it was who they were. Whether it was kicking a soccer ball in the backyard until sunset, playing with neighborhood friends, or analyzing every game on TV, sports shaped everything.

As we grew, we realized that our love for sports went beyond just playing. We found ourselves understanding what made teams successful, appreciating the business side of athletics, and noticing how the right equipment could transform someone's performance. We saw how proper gear could prevent injuries and how access to quality sporting goods could mean the difference between giving up and pushing forward.

Why AeroStride Exists

AeroStride was born from a simple realization—premium sports gear should be accessible, innovative, and designed with real athletes in mind. We saw athletes frustrated by generic fits, overpriced equipment, and products that didn't deliver on their promises.

We wanted to create something different: a brand where precision engineering meets personalized fit. Where advice comes from real experience and every athlete—whether taking their first steps or competing at elite levels—feels understood and supported.

That's why we're here: to make high-performance sports footwear more accessible, personal, and inspiring for athletes everywhere.

What Sets Us Apart

Athletic Heritage

We've played multiple sports throughout our lives, with particular passion for soccer, basketball, and running. We understand equipment from an athlete's perspective—we know what works, what doesn't, and why it matters.

Custom Fit Technology

Our 3D scanning technology creates truly personalized footwear. No more settling for standard sizes—experience gear designed exclusively for your unique foot geometry.

Innovation First

The sports industry evolves constantly. We stay ahead with aerospace-grade materials, carbon fiber propulsion systems, and advanced cushioning technology that delivers real performance gains.

Sustainable Excellence

We combine athletic passion with responsible business practices—ethical sourcing, environmental consciousness, and community investment—to create a brand that serves athletes and the planet long-term.

Genuine Partnership

This isn't just business for us. Every day, we get to help athletes pursue their passions, push their limits, and achieve their goals. That enthusiasm drives everything we do.

The Ultimate Goal

Our vision is ambitious: to revolutionize athletic footwear through precision engineering and custom fit technology. A brand where everyone—from youth league players to professional athletes—can find their perfect shoe and unlock their full potential.

But beyond business success, we want to contribute to a more active, healthy, and competitive sports community. We want to see more young athletes reaching their potential, more weekend warriors staying injury-free, and more people discovering the joy and discipline that athletics brings to life.

AeroStride is our way of turning a lifelong passion into something meaningful—not just for us, but for every athlete who shares that love of sports and the relentless pursuit of excellence.
